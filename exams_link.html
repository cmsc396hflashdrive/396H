Do UMD Students Plug in Flashdrives They Find?

This flash drive is a part of a study by Max Morrison, Michael Shultz, and Rakha Wibisana. Though there is no dangerous virus on this drive, other drives you find on the ground may have them. Please be careful plugging unknown hardware into your computer.
